The Perfect Tuna Sandwich

Description

This tuna sandwich is my favorite sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
  • 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, shredded
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon sour cream
  • ½ teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ⅛ teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • ¾ cup finely chopped onion

Directions

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover with cold water. Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat. Cover and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from hot water, cool, peel, and chop finely.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine tuna, crabmeat, mayonnaise, sour cream, and mustard. Stir in chopped egg, dill weed, lemon pepper, dill, sweet relish, and chopped onion. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Serve
